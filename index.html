My Favourite Animals

Elephants: Gentle Giants

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and remarkable memory. Native to Africa and Asia, they use their versatile trunks for various tasks. Despite their strength, elephants face threats like habitat loss and poaching, making conservation efforts vital for their survival.

Horses: Graceful Companions

Horses are elegant, powerful animals known for their speed, endurance, and close relationship with humans. They have been companions in work, sport, and leisure for centuries. With their keen intelligence and strong instincts, horses continue to inspire and play vital roles in cultures around the world.
